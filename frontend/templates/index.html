PRIVACY POLICY

Effective: June 2026

Information We Collect

We collect your email address and password (hashed with SHA-256) when you create an account. We automatically collect your IP address, device OS, and browser user agent to detect abuse and understand platform usage.

How We Use Your Data

Your data authenticates your account and syncs your download history across devices. We may send in-app notifications and promotional messages you can dismiss at any time. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Data Storage

All account and event data is stored in Cloudflare Workers KV — encrypted at rest on Cloudflare's global network. Event logs are automatically purged after 90 days.

Analytics

We log download completions, searches, and login events. These logs include IP address and device type but are never shared externally or sold.

Cookies & Tokens

We store an authentication token in your browser's localStorage to keep you signed in across sessions. No advertising cookies or third-party trackers are used.

Your Rights

You may request full deletion of your account and all data by emailing privacy@streamripper.app. Core download features work without an account.

Children's Privacy

StreamRipper is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
